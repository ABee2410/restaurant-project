Our Staff

In house professionals who keep us running and customize flavors and make our ice cream.

Kelly Davis is an intern here at Frozen Fusion and is working on her graduate degree in business.Her primary focus is in small business. Here, she helps us publicize as well as keeps things in order. From schedules to hiring, she coordinates us and keeps us flowing. Kelly also helps the kitchen staff when needed and is an official taste tester. Her recommended flavor is Key Lime Pie with Salted Carmel.

Lisette Bonavere is our main baker here at Frozen Fusion. A graduate from Le Cordon Bleu, Lissette is very experienced and only serves perfection. She is in charge of the preparation and baking of all the components in the ice cream from pies to cake batter to pastries, Lissette makes it all. One of her favorite things to make is the Birthday Cake ice cream because of the uniqueness in each batch she makes.
